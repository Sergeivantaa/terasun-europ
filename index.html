Composition

Portland cement

Primary binder providing structural rigidity and dimensional stability under load and temperature variation.

Cellulose fiber

Reinforcement matrix improving tensile strength and impact resistance. Asbestos-free formulation.

Silica aggregate

Filler contributing to density, compressive strength, and surface hardness of the finished board.

Autoclaved curing

High-pressure steam curing ensures permanent hydration and eliminates post-installation shrinkage.

For contractors & installers

Easy to install. Built for the job site.

Terasun TSM is designed for efficient installation with standard tools and framing systems used across European construction sites.

Lightweight handling

Logistics & supply chain

From factory to your warehouse

Terasun TSM boards are manufactured in China and supplied to European buyers via direct container shipment. Terasun Europe coordinates the full supply chain on your behalf.

01

Manufacturing (China)

Production at Zhejiang Terasun factory. Lead time from order confirmation: typically 3–5 weeks depending on volume and specification.

02

Container loading & export documentation

Boards palletised, shrink-wrapped, loaded into 20 ft or 40 ft containers. Full export documentation: packing list, invoice, CE certificates, bill of lading.

03

Ocean freight to Europe

Transit to European ports (Hamburg, Rotterdam, Helsinki, Tallinn, Gdańsk): approximately 4–6 weeks. Terasun Europe works with established freight forwarders.

04

EU customs clearance

Import documentation, customs duties, and CE compliance managed by Terasun Europe or our nominated customs broker.

05

Delivery to distributor warehouse

Container delivered to your warehouse after port clearance. Inland transport arranged by Terasun Europe or handled by the distributor from the nominated port.

06

European stock (under evaluation)

A central European stock point is under evaluation for 2026–2027, enabling smaller replenishment orders with shorter lead times. Partners will be notified when available.

Estimated total lead time: From order confirmation to delivery in Central Europe — approximately 10–14 weeks. Plan initial orders well in advance of your sales season.
